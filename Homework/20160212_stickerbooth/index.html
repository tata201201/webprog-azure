  • Click camera to take a photo.
  • Drag photo from the right to the photo to insert sticker.
  • Click sticker on the photo to select or deselect.
  • Press [arrowkeys] to move the selected sticker.
  • Press [-] or [+] key to resize the selected sticker.
  • Press [SHIFT] + [left arrowkey] or [right arrowkey] to rotate the selected sticker.
  • Press [DELETE] or [d] to delete the selected sticker.
  • If you want to save the photo, printscreen by yourself! This application cannot save image!
